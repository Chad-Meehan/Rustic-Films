THIS IS RUSTIC FILMS

Rustic Films is a production studio run by George Meehan in Christchurch, New Zealand.

George is a young film maker and student with a passion for all things film, and has been producing short films and commercial work for over 10 years.

Rustic Films aim is to move you and engross you in the stories of life.
